## Featured portfolio

Four repositories that together cover the core People Analytics value chain: workforce planning, recruiting, pay equity, and retention.

### [workforce-planning-demand-forecast](https://github.com/Jott2121/workforce-planning-demand-forecast)
Strategic workforce planning, recruiter capacity forecasting, and scenario-based hiring strategy. Monthly demand forecast with Base / Growth / Flat / Constrained scenarios, recruiter capacity model, clearance-weighted time-to-fill, ranked bottleneck detection, and an executive summary for leadership review. Five-page Streamlit dashboard. Calibrated for defense and regulated-industry hiring, including TS/SCI clearance premiums.

### [hiring-funnel-analytics](https://github.com/Jott2121/hiring-funnel-analytics)
Recruiting funnel performance, conversion analysis, source effectiveness, and demographic pass-rate monitoring. Two-proportion z-tests for pass-rate divergence at each funnel gate, budget-reallocation scatter, stage-level bias breakdown. Three-page Streamlit dashboard. [Live demo](https://hiring-funnel-analytics-jotterson.streamlit.app/).

### [compensation-equity-analysis](https://github.com/Jott2121/compensation-equity-analysis)
Regression-based pay equity audit following the methodology used by Mercer, WTW, and Aon. Two-stage analysis that isolates unexplained pay variation after controlling for level, function, location, tenure, experience, performance, and management status. Employee-level remediation planning with budget-aware funding curves. Three-page Streamlit dashboard. [Live demo](https://compensation-equity-jotterson.streamlit.app/).

### [hr-attrition-predictor](https://github.com/Jott2121/hr-attrition-predictor)
Responsible retention-risk modeling with per-individual SHAP explainability. Three benchmarked models (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost) on the IBM HR Analytics dataset, workforce-level risk dashboard, and conversation-ready driver attributions for HR business partners. Three-page Streamlit dashboard. [Live demo](https://hr-attrition-predictor-jotterson.streamlit.app/).

## Technical themes across the portfolio

- **Tableau / Power BI / Looker support** — exported CSVs and dashboard recipes in every repo for teams that standardize on BI platforms
